#### Formatting Guidelines

- The list is categorized by graduation year and sorted alphabetically (ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ).
- If the student received both their undergrad and graduate degree at Hampton, they will be identified by their undergrad graduation year.
- The student's name links to their social network (usually <a title="LinkedIn" href="http://linkedin.com" target="_blank">LinkedIn</a>).
- If a link is broken, please let someone from the ACM@HU team know.
- Last updated April 6, 2015.
