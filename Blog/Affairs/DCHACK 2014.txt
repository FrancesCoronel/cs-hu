# DCHACK 2014 

So this year, [CS@HU](http://facebook.com/CSHamptonU) bought a total **43** students to DCHack 2014, a hackathon that was hosted by Google at their office in Washington, DC. Each team had to have a minimum of 3 people with a maximum of 5 members.
